About

Ams is an internet platform developed to provide solution to the problem of distribution and sales of fresh farm produce. It is built to form a digital market where people can easily get straight off the farm fresh products delivered to them at any location. Get connected with farmers all over Nigeria, we know the importance of trust so you get to see whatever product you wish to buy as it is in the farm also with the time of harvest. Here we give upmost satisfaction to our users, covering logistics and delivering quality, cheap farm produce in less time and in whatever quantity and size required. Get the best of all farm products, rice, beans, palm oil including live animals, from the best farms around you.
